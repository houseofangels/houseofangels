Donations

You can help our women

The grand majority of women entering our program lack any means of support; usually their basic human needs are not covered. You can be a part of the women’s success story by contributing your time and resources to our organization.

How You Can Help… Donate material goods.

  • Food
  • Clothes, shoes
  • Toiletries
  • Bedding
  • Household items
  • Local transportation services

Other Ways To Help… Volunteer:

  • Teach Cooking Skills
  • Crafts, Beauty & Spirituality
  • Financial Management
  • Employment Workshops

We support our women throughout the reintegration process into society. It is important for us that they have hope and can live normal lives again. This will be possible with your help! If you are ready to help, please feel free to send us an email or call us.

Thank you!
